Opening

Who Was Mother Teresa, Really?

This session introduces the key theme briefly, then lets Mother Teresa answer in her own words through recorded interviews.

  • Not merely a symbol of kindness, but a radical disciple.
  • Deeply loved, widely criticized, and often misunderstood.
  • Uniquely relevant because we can still hear her directly.
“We will let her speak for herself.”
Life and calling

Brief Timeline

Her holiness unfolded in the modern world, not in a distant century. Her mission grew from obedience, not institutional strategy.

  • 1910: Born in Skopje
  • 1928-1929: Joins Sisters of Loreto
  • 1946: Receives the 'call within a call'
  • 1948: Enters the slums
  • 1950: Founding of Missionaries of Charity
  • 1979: Nobel Peace Prize
  • 1997: Dies in Kolkata
